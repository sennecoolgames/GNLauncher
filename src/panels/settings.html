Your RAM Settings
Adjust the amount of RAM allocated to your game. A larger amount will enhance your game's performance. Note that setting it too high might lead to performance loss in other applications.

- Maximum RAM: Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It's recommended not to exceed 50% of the total RAM on your PC for proper functioning. If your computer has over 8GB of RAM, we suggest allocating 3GB. If you have 4GB or less, do not allocate more than 2GB.

- Minimum RAM: Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated when your game starts. It's recommended not to set this value above 3GB. The typical value is 1GB.
